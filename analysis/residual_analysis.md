# Residual Analysis

## Predicted Sales
Predicted sales would normally be calculated using the final regression model.

## Residuals
Residual = Actual Monthly Sales − Predicted Monthly Sales

## Largest Positive Residuals
The records with the largest positive residuals represent stores that performed better than predicted by the model.

## Largest Negative Residuals
The records with the largest negative residuals represent stores that performed worse than predicted by the model.

## Business Interpretation
Positive residuals may indicate successful local marketing, seasonal demand, or other business factors not included in the model.

Negative residuals may indicate operational issues, increased competition, poor inventory management, or other external factors.

Because only simple regression models were completed, a full residual ranking was not generated.
